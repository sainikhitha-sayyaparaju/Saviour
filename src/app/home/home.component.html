What is SAVIOUR?

Saviour is a web app which helps fish and shrimp cultivators to know the weather conditions of their pond's locality and check if it they are suitable for the type of specie they are cultivating. All that we need is just your pond's location and the type of aqua fish or shrimp you are cultivating. We will acknowledge you whether the temperature is suitable or not.
Saviour is a best partner for fish and shrimp field Cultivators.
